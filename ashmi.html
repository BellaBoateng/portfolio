Ashmi Health

A brand and digital experience project focused on reimagining Ashmi Health’s identity, improving clarity, and creating a more cohesive and user-centered presence.

Role: UX/UI + Branding Tools: Figma, Canva, Research Type: Client Project

Overview

A clearer, more human-centered health experience.

This project focused on refining Ashmi Health’s brand identity and digital presence. The goal was to improve usability, simplify communication, and create a design system that reflects trust, clarity, and accessibility in a healthcare context.

Goal

“Design a health experience that feels clear, trustworthy, and approachable.”
