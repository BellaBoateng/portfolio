Book Club

A digital reading space designed around book discovery, reviews, and community-centered discussion.

Role: Designer + Developer Tools: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Figma Type: Personal Project

Overview

A cozy digital reading space.

Book Club is a web project designed to make reading feel more personal, visual, and community-centered. The site creates space for users to browse books, explore genres, view reviews, and eventually join discussion-based features.

Goal

“Make book discovery feel warm, interactive, and organized.”
