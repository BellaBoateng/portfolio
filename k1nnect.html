K1NNECT

A mentorship and community-building app designed to help first-generation college students feel more supported, connected, and confident.

Role: UX Research + UI Design Tools: Figma, Interviews, Prototyping Type: HCI Group Project

Overview

A support system for first-generation students.

K1NNECT was created to address isolation, lack of guidance, and limited access to campus resources among first-generation college students. The app combines mentorship, peer community, event discovery, and SkillSwap to help students build confidence and belonging.

Goal

“Help first-gen students connect, learn, and belong.”

Reflection

What this project taught me.

This project strengthened my understanding of human-centered design and the importance of grounding digital products in real user needs. Through interviews, personas, prototyping, and testing, I learned how research insights can directly shape interface decisions and create more supportive experiences for specific communities.
